College was founded in 1967 as a campus of the Cambrain College in Sudbury. The Canadore College is an applied arts and technology college found in North Bay, Ontario, Canada. The College became independent in 1972 and has grown to have four campuses in the Northen Bay area of Ontario and a campus in Parry Sound, Ontario.
