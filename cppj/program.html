Culinary Skills - Chef Training

The Canadore College Culinary Skills Chef Training Program helps to start your professional chef career! Train in their cutting-edge production kitchen. Learn Skills ranging from Food preparation, presentation and kitchen management in a real world setting. Have the chance to work in a world-class resort, on a cruise ship, or go into a business for yourself! Modern culinary skils and techniques will be aquired throughout buffet work. Tuition only costs $4,654.33 per year! $1234.09 for Ancillary Fees, $500 Material Fee, $200 Trip fee and a Tuition fee of $2720.24 Program only lasts for a short 2 years with Co-op Options.

  • 4. Food preparation and culinary skills
